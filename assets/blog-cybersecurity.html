Cybersecurity Tips for Beginners

Simple Steps to Stay Safe Online

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
  • Keep your software and devices updated.
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links.
  • Regularly back up important data.
  • Use antivirus and firewall protection.
  • Educate yourself about common cyber threats.

Cybersecurity is everyone's responsibility. By following these simple tips, you can protect yourself and your data from most online threats.
